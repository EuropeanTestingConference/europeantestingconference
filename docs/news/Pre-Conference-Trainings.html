Pre-Conference Trainings

author: Maaret Pyhäjärvi

The two-day European Testing Conference brings to Helsinki a lot of amazing speakers - over 30 names around the globe. We’re giving you a chance to select to spend more time with some of them on full day pre-conference trainings. You can acquire training tickets separately, or in cheaper in a bundle with the conference ticket.

We have 7 amazing full day pre-conference trainings for you to select from. The trainings are delivered by:

Fiona Charles (@FionaCCharles)

Kevlin Henney (@KevlinHenney) teaches Software Craftmanship full-day class.

Liz Keogh (@Lunivore)

Angie Jones (@techgirl1908) teaches Starting Out with Selenium full-day class.

Gitte Klitgaard (@nativewired) & Aki Salmi (@rinkkasatiainen) teach a shared full-day class Effective Communication.

Maaret Pyhäjärvi (@maaretp) teaches Mob Testing, for both exploratory and automated tasks on a full-day class.

Matt Lavoie (@MattPLavoie) & Daniel Billing (@theTestDoctor) do half-day each on the theme of two most important parafunctional areas - Usability and Security.

Join us for enjoying their messages and many others in Helsinki, Finland in February 2017! Tickets are available with Early Bird Prices (550 euros + VAT for a day of training or two days of conference) until end of October.

You can reach organizers at contact@europeantestingconference.eu
